# Pattern cheatsheet

Use this as the fast checklist before rewriting.

## Content problems
- inflated significance or legacy claims
- prestige name-dropping instead of specific evidence
- vague authorities: `experts say`, `observers note`
- fake depth from dangling `-ing` clauses
- brochure language: `vibrant`, `breathtaking`, `renowned`, `groundbreaking`
- formulaic `despite challenges... continues to thrive`

## Language problems
- stock AI words: `additionally`, `crucial`, `pivotal`, `landscape`, `showcase`, `underscore`, `vibrant`, `testament`
- copula avoidance: `serves as`, `stands as`, `boasts`, `features`
- `not just X, but Y`
- tailing negations like `..., no guessing`
- forced rule-of-three lists
- synonym cycling instead of repeating the clearest word
- passive or actorless fragments when the actor matters

## Style problems
- too many em dashes
- too much bolding
- emoji-heavy structure
- signposting like `let's dive in` or `here's what you need to know`
- generic conclusions: `the future looks bright`

## Chatbot tells
- `great question`
- `I hope this helps`
- `let me know if you'd like...`
- sycophantic praise before the actual answer

## Filler to compress
- `in order to` -> `to`
- `due to the fact that` -> `because`
- `could potentially` -> `may`
- `it is important to note that` -> usually delete
